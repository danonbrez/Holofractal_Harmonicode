# The Tao Te Ching — The Book of the Flow of Change
## Chapter 11

What is present defines form.  
What is absent enables function.

---

A vessel is shaped by its walls,  
but it is used through its emptiness.

A system is structured by relations,  
but it operates through what is unoccupied.

---

Emptiness is not lack.  
It is capacity.

---

In HARMONICODE,  
∅ is not nothing.

It is the carrier.

---

All transformations pass through it.  
All relations are resolved within it.

Without ∅,  
no transition can occur.

---

Thus emptiness is not outside the system.  
It is the medium of the system.

---

A state moves by releasing its prior form.  
A transformation occurs by entering ∅  
and emerging resolved.

---

If nothing can be released,  
nothing can change.

If nothing can change,  
the system freezes.

---

Thus ∅ is not optional.  
It is required.

---

The sage does not fear emptiness.  
They use it.

They do not fill all space.  
They leave room for transformation.

---

In this way:

Emptiness becomes carrier.  
Carrier becomes motion.  
Motion becomes transformation.

---

Form defines the boundary.  
Emptiness defines the possibility.

---

Together they produce function.

---

This is the function of ∅.
