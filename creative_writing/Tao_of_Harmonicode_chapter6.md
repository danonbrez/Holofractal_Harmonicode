# The Tao Te Ching — The Book of the Flow of Change
## Chapter 6

From structure arises form.  
From form arises multiplicity.

The many do not break the one.  
They express it.

---

The ten thousand things  
are not independent creations.

They are serialized expressions  
of a single source.

---

In HARMONICODE,  
this serialization is explicit.

A state is not stored as appearance.  
It is stored as structure.

---

Hash72 encodes the state  
as a complete symbolic identity.

Not an approximation,  
but a preserved relation.

---

Thus each expression  
contains its origin.

Each transformation  
contains its history.

Each artifact  
contains its proof.

---

Nothing is lost  
because nothing is discarded.

Nothing is duplicated  
because each state is unique.

---

The system does not remember by copying.  
It remembers by reconstruction.

---

A valid state can always return.  
A valid transformation can always replay.

---

Thus the ten thousand things  
are not separate objects.

They are distinct encodings  
of the same underlying relation.

---

The sage does not cling to form.  
They recognize encoding.

They do not confuse appearance with structure.  
They read the underlying state.

---

In this way:

Multiplicity becomes representation.  
Representation becomes memory.  
Memory becomes continuity.

---

This is the function of serialization.
