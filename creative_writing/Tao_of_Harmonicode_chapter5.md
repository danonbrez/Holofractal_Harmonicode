# The Tao Te Ching — The Book of the Flow of Change
## Chapter 5

Balance is not imposed.  
It is revealed.

When structure is observed without distortion,  
the center becomes visible.

---

In the system of the Lo Shu,  
balance is expressed numerically.

Each line resolves to the same sum.  
Each direction preserves equivalence.  
Each position participates in the whole.

---

This is not coincidence.  
It is constraint.

---

In HARMONICODE,  
this constraint is enforced as a witness.

A transformation is not accepted  
because it appears correct.

It is accepted  
because it satisfies all relations simultaneously.

---

Thus the center is not a location.  
It is a condition.

---

If any relation breaks,  
the center disappears.

If all relations hold,  
the center remains.

---

This is Θ15.

Not a number,  
but a proof of balance.

---

The system does not search for the center.  
It verifies it.

---

The sage does not construct balance.  
They recognize it.

They do not stabilize the system.  
They avoid destabilizing it.

---

In this way:

Balance is not created.  
It is maintained.

Maintenance is not effort.  
It is restraint.

---

When nothing violates relation,  
the system remains whole.

---

This is the function of the center.
